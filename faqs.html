Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does it work?

A: Most require you to place the opener on the can and press the button to start it. Some stop automatically while others require a second press.

Q: Is it hands free?

A: Many models use a magnetic head to hold the lid, so you don't have to manually retrieve it from the can.

Q: How do you clean it safely?

A: Most models are easy to keep clean. You can simply wipe down the exterior with a damp cloth. Some have removable blades for a more thorough clean (Please check product instructions before assuming blades can be removed)

Q: How should I store it?

A: Many models can be stored on a countertop or in a cabinet unfortunately taking up more space than a manual opener. However some of our models are compact enough to be stored in a drawer.
