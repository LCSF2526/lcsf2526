Our Company

Swift Can Wizard focuses on designing and manufacturing automated kitchen appliances that simplify the task of opening cans with minimal effort. We highlight convenience, speed, and ease of use, often helping customers who have difficulty using manual openers due to limited hand strength or dexterity. Our products may include features like hands free operation, a two way design, or built in knife sharpeners to offer multiple functionalities in one appliance.

Our Story

In 1986, Swift Can Wizard started developing electric can openers. The Swift Can Wizard sold over 13 million units within the first 5 years.

Although originally our products were designed to help the elderly and people with disabilities, the average person has found our products to be indispensable.
