Our Company


In 1986, Household Conveniences started developing electric can openers. After achieving a patent, the Can Wizard was born. The Can Wizard sold over 13 million units within the first 5 years. Since then, Household Conveniences has continued developing products to make life easier.

Although originally our products were designed to help the elderly and people with disabilities, the average person has found our products to be indispensable. Our leadership team is devoted to ensuring exceptional customer satisfaction and will continue to improve our products to provide the greatest convenience possible.

With various locations across North America reaching numbers upwards of 245, employing 15,000+ associates and hosting a convenient online presence, Household Conveniences is available to help at a moment's notice.
